From 2010 to 2018, I served as a volunteer with Home & Hope of San Mateo,
providing a temporary living space and comprehensive support for families
experiencing hardship.
Home & Hope merged with
<a href='https://lifemoves.org/home-hope-is-now-lifemoves/'><u>LifeMoves.org</u></a>
in 2023 to expand these services across San Mateo and Santa Clara Counties between
San Francisco and San Jose, California.
I was on the rotation list of overnight hosts to provide evening meals,
breakfasts, supervise children's activities, and handle overnight emergencies.
